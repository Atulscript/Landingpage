GS + CSAT PRE CUM MAIN FOUNDATION COURSE

The NEXT IAS GS Pre cum Main Foundation course is a comprehensive, 10-month program designed to prepare students for the UPSC Civil Services Examination, including the Civil Services Aptitude Test (CSAT). The course is taught by experienced faculty and includes regular classes, doubt-clearing sessions, and intensive sessions on writing skills. The curriculum is well-planned and includes current affairs, newspaper analysis, and interactive learning methods such as audio-visuals and PowerPoint presentations.The course also provides updated and revised study materials to supplement the lectures
